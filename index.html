Common questions

What does Rundock cost?

Rundock is free. You will need a Claude Pro ($20/mo) or Max ($100/mo) subscription for the AI processing, since Rundock runs on top of Claude Code. That is the total cost.

Where does my data live?

On your machine. Rundock runs locally and never sends your files, agents, or conversations anywhere. Only the active conversation is sent to Anthropic for processing, via Claude Code.

Can I bring my context from ChatGPT, Notion, or Claude?

Your workspace is plain markdown files. If you have notes elsewhere, copy them into your workspace and your agents can read them straight away. No import tool, no migration step.

What if Rundock stops being maintained?

Your workspace is yours. Agents and skills are markdown files you own. The code is open source. Worst case, you keep using whatever version works on your machine, or fork it, or take your agents to any tool that supports the Claude agent format.

How long until I am up and running?

Around an evening. Install Claude Code (10 minutes). Download Rundock and pick a workspace folder (5 minutes). Talk to Doc and let him build your first three agents (30 to 60 minutes). You are running.
